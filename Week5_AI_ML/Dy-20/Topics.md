# Artificial Intelligence Basics

---

# 1. Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Artificial Intelligence means making machines smart enough to do tasks that normally need human intelligence.

Humans can:

- Learn
- Understand
- Make decisions
- Solve problems

AI tries to give these abilities to machines.

---

## Simple Understanding

```text
Artificial = Man-made
Intelligence = Ability to think and learn
```

So,

> AI = Man-made intelligence inside machines.

---

# How AI Works

AI mainly works in 3 steps:

---

## 1. Input

AI takes data like:

- Images
- Text
- Voice
- Videos

---

## 2. Learning

AI studies patterns from the data.

---

## 3. Output

AI gives results or decisions.

### Examples

- Detecting faces
- Answering questions
- Recommending videos

---

# Real-Life Examples of AI

| Application | Work Done by AI |
|---|---|
| ChatGPT | Answers questions |
| YouTube | Recommends videos |
| Google Maps | Finds best route |
| Face Unlock | Recognizes face |
| Siri/Alexa | Understands voice |

---

# 2. Machine Learning (ML)

Machine Learning is a part of AI.

In ML, machines learn automatically from data instead of following only fixed instructions.

---

## Simple Definition

> ML is teaching machines to learn from data and improve with experience.

---

# Example of ML

Suppose we want to detect spam emails.

Instead of writing thousands of rules manually:

- We give many spam and non-spam emails
- ML finds patterns itself
- Then it predicts future spam emails

---

# ML Process

```text
Data → Training → Learning → Prediction
```

---

# Features of ML

- Learns from data
- Improves accuracy over time
- Finds hidden patterns
- Gives predictions

---

# Examples of ML

- Netflix recommendations
- Spam detection
- Online shopping suggestions
- Weather prediction

---

# 3. Deep Learning (DL)

Deep Learning is an advanced part of Machine Learning.

It uses neural networks with many layers to learn complex things deeply.

DL is inspired by the human brain.

---

## Simple Definition

> Deep Learning is advanced machine learning using neural networks.

---

# Why “Deep” Learning?

Because it learns through multiple layers:

- First layer learns simple things
- Next layers learn more detailed patterns

### Example

In image recognition:

- First layer detects edges
- Next detects shapes
- Final layer recognizes the object

---

# Examples of DL

- Voice assistants
- Self-driving cars
- Image recognition
- ChatGPT and LLMs
- Video generation AI

---

# Relationship Between AI, ML, and DL

```text
AI → ML → DL
```

- AI is the biggest field
- ML is inside AI
- DL is inside ML

# Types of AI

AI is classified in two ways:

1. Based on Capability  
2. Based on Functionality  

---

# A. Types of AI Based on Capability

---

## 1. Narrow AI (Weak AI)

This AI is made for one specific task only.

It cannot perform tasks outside its training.

---

### Features

- Limited capability
- Task-specific
- Most common AI today

---

### Examples

- ChatGPT
- Google Assistant
- Face recognition
- Recommendation systems

---

### Simple Understanding

> Narrow AI is smart in only one area.

---

## 2. General AI (Strong AI)

General AI can think and learn like humans.

It can:

- Understand multiple tasks
- Use logic
- Make decisions
- Learn new things

---

### Current Status

Still not fully developed.

---

### Simple Understanding

> General AI can do almost everything a human can do.

---

## 3. Super AI

Super AI is AI that becomes smarter than humans.

It may:

- Solve problems faster
- Think better than humans
- Improve itself

---

### Current Status

Only theoretical right now.

---

### Simple Understanding

> Super AI is intelligence beyond humans.
